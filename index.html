College Profile

Maturi Venkata Subba Rao (MVSR) Engineering College was established in 1981 with affiliation to Osmania University. Currently, the college has B.E programs in Civil, CSE, ECE, EEE, IT, Mechanical and Automobile Engineering, PG programs in CSE, ECE and Mechanical besides MBA.
